Common questions

Who benefits most from a membership?
Any homeowner who wants to stay ahead of plumbing problems rather than react to them. That includes people who just purchased a home and want to understand what they've got, longtime owners focused on maintenance, and landlords managing multiple properties. The 10% discount applies to every service call — members with even one or two service needs per year typically recoup the cost of the membership.
What does the 65-point inspection cover?
A licensed plumber walks through your entire home with you and visually inspects every plumbing element: bathroom faucets, tubs, showers, and toilets; kitchen sinks and dishwasher connections; garbage disposals; laundry hookups; exterior hose bibs; sump and ejector pumps; and water heaters. We answer your questions as we go, and at the end we provide a written report with any recommendations — so you leave with a clear picture of your home's plumbing health.
Can I buy memberships for multiple properties?
Yes. Each membership applies to a single address. If you'd like coverage across multiple properties — a second home, or a rental portfolio — you can purchase individual memberships for each. Many of our landlord clients do exactly this.
When do my benefits start?
Immediately. Your 10% discount is active the moment you purchase and applies to any service call — including the same day you sign up.
How do I sign up?
Call our office at 773-617-3686, Monday through Friday, 7am–4pm. Our plumbers can also set you up on-site during a service visit if you'd like to get started right away.
